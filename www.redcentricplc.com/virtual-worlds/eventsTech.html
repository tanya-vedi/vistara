LFR

You have to follow one path to achieve your goal –

but after reaching the specific point, the path changes as per the goal. Be an innovator and walk on the lines of change. Innerve’17 presents the LFR challenge, make your Bot complete the course in the shortest period of time while accurately tracking the course line from start to finish.Specifications of LFR:
These rounds are based in a line following robot. It will comprises of a track laid in black strip on white sheet, having a starting & finishing line with 3-5 intermediate check points
Robot will have to follow the black line from start to finish for successful completion of the track .width of black strip is nearly 1.5-2 inches.
The robot should fit in a square of 25*25cm
For further details, mail us at innerve-techevents@gmail.com
